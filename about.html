About Wick & Essence

Wick & Essence was created around a simple idea: scent and light shape the way we experience our spaces.

Traditional candles and diffusers exist separately, but Wick & Essence combines them into one elegant system. By allowing interchangeable scent cartridges and wax inserts, the goal is to create a flexible home fragrance experience that can adapt to different moods, seasons, and environments.

Whether it's a calm evening at home, a productive workspace, or a welcoming atmosphere for guests, Wick & Essence is designed to help people intentionally shape the feeling of their space.
